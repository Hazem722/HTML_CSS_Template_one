About

Less is more work

Founded in 1993 by owners Rick Hilton and Jeff Hyland, Hilton & Hyland has remained privately-held, steadfastly independent, and unfettered by investors or outside shareholders for nearly 30 years. With our sole office in Beverly Hills, we are perhaps the top-producing boutique brokerage in the world.

Hilton & Hyland once again led the luxury residential market in Los Angeles in 2020 with a 45% market share of sales priced $20 million and above, a 29% market share of sales priced $10 million and above, and a 23% market share of sales priced $5 million and above. On top of that, our annual sales volume hit $3.5 billion with an average price per transaction of $5.9 million.
